About Us

Company Information
At AcruX, we make handcrafted classic watches with centuries-old knowledge. AcruX watches were first invented in the mid-15th century. The knowledge of watchmaking has been passed down for 19 generations and will continue. The founder's name of our company is Crucis Bond. Inspired by his name, which is the name of a star in the constellation Crux, he named the company AcruX, which is the brightest star of the Crux constellation.

Mission and Vision
Our goal is to revive the beauty of classic watches by adding modern features while maintaining pure handcrafted traditional designs. Due to the trend of minimalism and practicality, many items have lost their soul. Our aim is to bring back aesthetic items.
