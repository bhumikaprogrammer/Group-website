Detailed Analysis

Samaya Watch: Samaya Watch site uses high quality imagery, minimal design, and a focus on their (sustainable sourced and made) Nepali watches. It is easy for users to explore the products when the layout is simple and colors neutral. It's really easy to navigate, and all the responsive elements work seamlessly on desktop and mobile devices. The site speed and efficiency is also achieved by using fancy tecnologies like HTML 5 and Javascript, but no fancy things like live chat, advanced filters, etc.

Titan: Dynamic and colourful Titan Web site reflects its worldwide audience and product portfolio. To do so, it uses beautiful photographs, colourful banners, and personalised sections that highlight curated collections and events. It's not hard to browse a huge catalog, like an intuitive interface with general filters and a search function. The website built with modern frameworks (e.g. React. js)for adaptation and offers a flexible interface across devices. Small things like this: customer reviews, advanced payment options, detailed FAQ fall into the usability category.
